<p><p>The manufacturing industrial casters market primarily includes two types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters feature a rotating mounting system that allows for enhanced maneuverability, making them ideal for transporting loads in tight spaces. In contrast, rigid casters have a fixed wheel orientation, providing stability and straight-line movement, which is beneficial for heavy-duty applications. Together, these casters serve diverse industrial needs, facilitating easier movement of equipment and materials in warehouses, factories, and other industrial settings.</p></p>
