<p><ul><li>Skewed Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire market primarily includes skewed and radial tire types. Skewed tires, often known as bias tires, feature layers of fabric plies arranged at angles, providing durability and a flexible ride suitable for off-road applications. In contrast, radial tires have plies arranged perpendicular to the tread, offering better grip, improved fuel efficiency, and enhanced handling on highways. Both types serve different vehicle needs, with radial tires gaining popularity for passenger vehicles while skewed tires remain relevant in specialized and heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
