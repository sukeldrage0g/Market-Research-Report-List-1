<p><p>The gluten-free beer market is primarily divided into two types: dedicated gluten-free beers and gluten-reduced beers. Dedicated gluten-free beers are brewed from gluten-free grains, ensuring that they contain no gluten, making them suitable for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. In contrast, gluten-reduced beers utilize traditional gluten-containing grains but undergo a process to reduce gluten levels, making them potentially safer for some gluten-sensitive individuals, though they may still contain trace amounts of gluten.</p></p>
