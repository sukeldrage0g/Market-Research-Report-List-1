<p><p>The Electro Optical System market is primarily divided into two types: Infrared and Laser. Infrared systems utilize thermal imaging to detect heat signatures, making them ideal for surveillance, search and rescue, and nighttime operations. They operate effectively in low-light conditions. Laser systems, on the other hand, employ focused light to achieve high precision in targeting, measurement, and communication. They are widely used in military applications, industrial automation, and precision guidance, offering enhanced accuracy and operational capabilities.</p></p>
