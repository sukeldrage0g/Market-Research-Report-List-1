<p><strong>The Mechanical Grates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scroll Type</li><li>Reciprocating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical grates market is primarily classified into two types: scroll type and reciprocating type. Scroll type grates feature a continuous, spiral design that effectively transports and processes materials, optimizing efficiency in combustion applications. In contrast, reciprocating type grates utilize a back-and-forth motion to move fuel, allowing for greater versatility in handling various solid wastes. Both types are essential in waste-to-energy and biomass power plants, enhancing combustion efficiency and reducing emissions while accommodating different fuel characteristics.</p></p>
