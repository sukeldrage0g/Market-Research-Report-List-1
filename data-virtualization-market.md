<p><p>Data virtualization market is primarily divided into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise data virtualization involves deploying software within an organization’s infrastructure, allowing for greater control, security, and compliance with data governance policies. In contrast, cloud-based data virtualization offers flexibility and scalability, enabling users to access and integrate data from multiple sources via the internet, often at a lower cost. Both types aim to enhance data accessibility and promote real-time analytics without physical data movement.</p></p>
