<p><p>Plant Substation Automation Systems (SAS) enhance the efficiency and reliability of electrical grid operations by integrating monitoring, control, and protection functionalities within substations. The market is segmented into transmission substations and distribution substations. Transmission substations focus on high-voltage power transfer over long distances, prioritizing system stability and security. In contrast, distribution substations manage the voltage reduction and distribution of electricity to end-users, emphasizing reliable service delivery and localized management of electrical resources. Both are essential for optimized energy management.</p></p>
