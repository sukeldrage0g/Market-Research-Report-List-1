<p><strong>The Distance Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-Optical Distance Meters</li><li>Laser Distance Meters</li><li>Sound Wave Distance Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distance meters market includes various technologies for measuring distances. Electro-optical distance meters use light waves and triangulation to determine distances, suitable for both short and long-range measurements. Laser distance meters utilize laser beams for precise distance calculations, offering high accuracy and quick results, ideal for construction and surveying. Sound wave distance meters employ ultrasonic sound waves to measure distance based on the time taken for sound to travel and return, commonly used in indoor applications like room measurements.</p></p>
