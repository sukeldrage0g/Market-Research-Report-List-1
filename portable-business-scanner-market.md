<p><p>The portable business scanner market is segmented into single-feed and multi-feed check scanners. Single-feed check scanners process one document at a time, making them suitable for low-volume use, such as small businesses handling occasional checks. In contrast, multi-feed check scanners can handle multiple documents simultaneously, catering to high-volume transactions and larger businesses that require efficient processing. Both types offer portability and convenience, enabling users to capture and digitize checks and documents quickly and effectively.</p></p>
