<p><strong>The Digital Dust Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Dust Meter</li><li>Electrostatic Dust Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital dust meter market comprises various types, notably optical and electrostatic dust meters. Optical dust meters utilize laser beams to measure particulate concentration in the air, providing real-time data on dust levels. They are known for their accuracy and quick response time. In contrast, electrostatic dust meters function by measuring the electrical charge of particles, making them effective for specific applications where charged dust particles are prevalent. Both types serve critical roles in air quality monitoring across industries.</p></p>
