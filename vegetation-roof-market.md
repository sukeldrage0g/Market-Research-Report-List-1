<p><p>The vegetation roof market is categorized into three main types: extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive. Extensive roofs feature lightweight, drought-resistant plants, requiring minimal maintenance and typically have shallow substrate layers. Semi-intensive roofs offer a broader variety of plants and deeper substrate for more diverse vegetation, striking a balance between maintenance and aesthetics. Intensive roofs, on the other hand, support a wide array of plants, including trees and shrubs, necessitating significant structural support and ongoing maintenance for rich landscapes.</p></p>
