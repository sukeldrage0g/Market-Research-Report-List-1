<p><p>The Hard Rock Shield Machine market comprises two primary types of Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs): shielded type and open type. Shielded type TBMs are used for tunneling in hard rock conditions while providing protection to the surrounding ground, enhancing stability and safety during the excavation process. Conversely, open type TBMs operate without an integrated shield and are typically utilized in less challenging geological formations. Both types play a crucial role in infrastructure development, particularly in underground construction projects.</p></p>
