<p><p>The fire and rescue helmets market is primarily divided into two types: structural helmets and jet-style helmets. Structural helmets are designed for firefighting in hazardous environments, offering enhanced protection against impacts, heat, and debris while providing face and neck coverage. Jet-style helmets, on the other hand, are more lightweight and streamlined, typically used by emergency service personnel in non-structural roles, focusing on ease of movement and visibility, making them ideal for rescue operations and general firefighting tasks.</p></p>
