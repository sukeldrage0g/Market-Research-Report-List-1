<p><p>The External Ventricular Drainage System market consists of various types, including valves, shunts, external drainage systems, and others. Valves regulate cerebral spinal fluid flow, ensuring optimal pressure management. Shunts divert fluid away from the brain to reduce intracranial pressure. External drainage systems facilitate the removal of excess fluid for monitoring and treatment purposes. Other categories may include accessories and specialized components, collectively supporting effective management of conditions like hydrocephalus and traumatic brain injuries.</p></p>
