<p><p>The cosmetic adhesive market is segmented into four main types: Epoxy Resin, Polyurethane, Acrylic, and Others. Epoxy resin adhesives offer strong bonding and durability, ideal for demanding applications. Polyurethane adhesives provide flexibility and resilience, making them suitable for skin contact products. Acrylic adhesives are known for their fast curing time and strong adhesion, often used in temporary applications. The 'Others' category includes specialized adhesives that cater to specific cosmetic needs, enhancing performance in various formulations and applications.</p></p>
