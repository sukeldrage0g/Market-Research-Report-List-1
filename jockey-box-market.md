<p><ul><li>Coil Cooler</li><li>Cold-plate Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jockey boxes are portable beer dispensing systems that utilize different cooling methods to maintain optimal beer temperatures during events. Coil coolers feature a coiled copper or stainless-steel line immersed in ice water, efficiently cooling the beer as it travels through the coils. Cold-plate coolers, on the other hand, use metal plates chilled in advance to cool the beer quickly as it flows through connected channels. Both types are popular in various settings, offering convenience for outdoor gatherings and parties.</p></p>
