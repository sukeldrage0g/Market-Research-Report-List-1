<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP</li><li>Pitch-based CFRTP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) composites market is primarily divided into two types based on the carbon fiber precursor: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based and Pitch-based CFRTP. PAN-based CFRTP is known for its high strength and modulus, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, Pitch-based CFRTP typically offers lower costs and good thermal properties, suitable for electrical and thermal applications. Both types contribute significantly to enhancing the mechanical performance and durability of thermoplastic materials.</p></p>
