<p><p>Fracking fluid and chemicals are essential for hydraulic fracturing, with various market types catering to specific needs. Water-based fluids, the most common, utilize water combined with additives to enhance performance and reduce environmental impact. Oil-gel based fluids, which use oil as a base, provide better viscosity and stability in high-temperature formations. Foam-based fluids combine gases with liquid, offering improved mobility and reduced water usage. Each type is tailored to optimize extraction efficiency and support specific geological conditions in oil and gas production.</p></p>
