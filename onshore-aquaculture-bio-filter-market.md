<p><p>The onshore aquaculture bio filter market is primarily categorized into mechanical and biological filters. Mechanical filters are designed to remove solid waste and particulate matter from the water, ensuring clarity and reducing the load on biological systems. In contrast, biological filters utilize beneficial microorganisms to break down harmful substances like ammonia and nitrites, thereby maintaining water quality and promoting healthy aquatic environments. Together, these filtration systems are essential for optimizing fish farming operations and enhancing sustainability in aquaculture practices.</p></p>
