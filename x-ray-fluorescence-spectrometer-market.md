<p><p>The X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometer (XRF) market comprises three main types: Energy Dispersive XRF (EDXRF), which provides rapid elemental analysis and is cost-effective; Wavelength Dispersive XRF (WD-XRF), known for its high resolution and sensitivity, ideal for trace element detection; and Polarized Energy Dispersive XRF, which enhances detection capabilities by using polarized X-rays, improving accuracy in complex matrices. Each type offers distinct advantages suitable for various applications, including materials testing, environmental analysis, and quality control in manufacturing.</p></p>
