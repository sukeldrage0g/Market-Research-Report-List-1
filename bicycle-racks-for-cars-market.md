<p><ul><li>Roof Bike Racks</li><li>Hitch Bike Racks</li><li>Trunk Bike Racks</li><li>Truck Bed Bike Racks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bicycle racks for cars come in several types, each catering to different needs. Roof bike racks attach to the vehicle's roof, offering a space-saving solution but requiring lifting the bike. Hitch bike racks connect to a trailer hitch and provide easy access, accommodating multiple bikes. Trunk bike racks secure to the rear of the vehicle, often more affordable and versatile for various cars. Truck bed bike racks fit into the truck's bed, providing stability and protection during transport.</p></p>
