<p><ul><li>AFIS Technology</li><li>Non-AFIS Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fingerprint biometrics market is divided into two primary types: AFIS (Automated Fingerprint Identification System) and Non-AFIS technology. AFIS technology is a comprehensive system used by law enforcement for identifying individuals through fingerprint matches against vast databases, enhancing security and investigation efficiency. Conversely, Non-AFIS technology pertains to simpler, often standalone systems used for access control and identification in various industries, focusing on real-time verification rather than extensive database comparisons. Both types serve distinct applications in biometric security.</p></p>
