<p><p>Asphalt roofing shingles are primarily categorized into two types: laminated shingles and three-tab shingles. Laminated shingles, also known as architectural shingles, feature multiple layers, providing enhanced durability and a dimensional appearance, making them popular for upscale homes. Three-tab shingles, on the other hand, consist of a single layer and have a flat, uniform profile, often being more cost-effective. Both types offer diverse styles and colors, catering to various aesthetic preferences and budget considerations in the roofing market.</p></p>
