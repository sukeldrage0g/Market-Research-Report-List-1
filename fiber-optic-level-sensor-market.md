<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic level sensor market is segmented into contact and contactless types. Contact level sensors utilize optical fibers that are directly in contact with the material being measured, providing precise measurements for liquids and solids. They are often employed in demanding environments. In contrast, contactless level sensors measure the level without physical contact, using light reflections or transmission to gauge levels. This type is ideal for applications where contamination or maintenance needs to be minimized.</p></p>
