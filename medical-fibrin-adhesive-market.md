<p><p>The medical fibrin adhesive market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Water-based** adhesives utilize aqueous solutions for application, offering biocompatibility and easy handling. </p><p>2. **Solvent-based** adhesives use volatile organic compounds, providing strong bonding but requiring careful handling due to potential toxicity. </p><p>3. **Solids** adhesives are formulated in solid forms, often needing activation before use, offering long shelf life and minimal handling issues. </p><p>4. **Hot Melt-based** adhesives are applied in a molten state, ensuring fast bonding and suitability for various surgical applications.</p></p>
