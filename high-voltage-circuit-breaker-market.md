<p><p>The High Voltage Circuit Breaker market is categorized into several types based on the medium used for interruption. Vacuum High Voltage Circuit Breakers utilize a vacuum to quench the arc, offering reliable performance and minimal maintenance. Sulfur Hexafluoride (SF6) Circuit Breakers use SF6 gas, providing excellent insulation and arc-extinguishing properties. Air Circuit Breakers rely on air for insulation and arc suppression, while Oil Circuit Breakers use mineral oil for insulation and cooling. Other types may include hybrids and emerging technologies.</p></p>
