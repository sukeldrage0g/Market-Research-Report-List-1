<p><strong>The Endoscopy Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Endoscopes</li><li>Rigid Endoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscopy device market is primarily segmented into flexible and rigid endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are designed with adaptable shafts, allowing them to navigate through curved paths in the body, making them ideal for minimal invasive procedures. They are widely used in gastrointestinal, respiratory, and urological applications. Rigid endoscopes, characterized by their stiff structure, are typically used in surgeries requiring direct visualization, such as orthopedic and ENT procedures. Both types contribute significantly to advancements in diagnostics and therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
