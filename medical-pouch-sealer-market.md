<p><ul><li>Pulse Sealer</li><li>Constant Heat Sealer</li><li>Continuous Sealer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical pouch sealer market includes three main types: Pulse Sealers, Constant Heat Sealers, and Continuous Sealers. Pulse Sealers operate using short bursts of heat to create high-strength seals, ideal for packaging sensitive medical devices. Constant Heat Sealers maintain a steady temperature for consistent sealing, suitable for thicker materials. Continuous Sealers facilitate the continuous sealing of pouches at high speeds, enhancing production efficiency. Each type caters to specific packaging requirements in the medical industry, ensuring product integrity and sterility.</p></p>
