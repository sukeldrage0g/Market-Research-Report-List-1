<p><p>The flaw detection instruments market encompasses various technologies for identifying defects in materials. Magnetic flaw detectors utilize magnetic fields to reveal surface and near-surface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials. Ultrasonic flaw detectors employ high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects, offering high accuracy and depth penetration. Other market types include eddy current and optical methods, each serving specific applications and materials. Together, these instruments ensure quality control across industries by enabling early detection of potential failures in materials.</p></p>
