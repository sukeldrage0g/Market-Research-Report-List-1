<p><p>The IGBT market consists of three primary types: IGBT Discretes, IGBT Modules, and IGBT Integrated Power Modules (IPM). IGBT Discretes are individual semiconductor devices used in various applications for switching and amplification. IGBT Modules combine multiple IGBTs and diodes in a single package, enhancing performance and thermal management for larger power applications. IGBT-IPMs integrate control, protection, and driver circuits with IGBT technology, offering compact solutions for easy integration in systems like motor drives and renewable energy converters.</p></p>
