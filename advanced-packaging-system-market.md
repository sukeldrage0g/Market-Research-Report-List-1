<p><p>The Advanced Packaging System market encompasses various innovative packaging technologies for semiconductors enhancing performance and miniaturization. 3.0 DIC (Die In Package) improves integration density. FO SIP (Fan-Out System in Package) allows for multi-die configurations with increased I/O. FO WLP (Fan-Out Wafer Level Package) offers a compact design for high-performance applications. 3D WLP and WLCSP (Wafer Level Chip Scale Package) provide stacked die solutions. 2.5D technology utilizes interposers for improved connectivity, while Flip Chip packaging enhances thermal and electrical performance through direct die attachment.</p></p>
