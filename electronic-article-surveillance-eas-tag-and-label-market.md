<p><strong>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Tag and Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard</li><li>Soft</li><li>Permanent Deactivation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) tag and label market includes three primary types: hard tags, soft tags, and permanent deactivation systems. Hard tags are robust devices typically used for high-value items, designed to be tamper-resistant and frequently removed at the point of sale. Soft tags are thinner and more flexible, ideal for apparel and easily deactivated at checkout. Permanent deactivation systems render tags inoperable, ensuring theft prevention while allowing the product to be sold without requiring tag removal.</p></p>
