<p><p>The telemetry market encompasses various types of systems for data transmission and monitoring. Wire-link or wired telemetry utilizes physical connections for reliable data transfer, while wireless telemetry systems offer flexibility and ease of installation without cables. Data loggers collect and store data from various sensors for later analysis. Acoustic telemetry uses sound waves to transmit information, particularly in underwater applications. Digital telemetry involves the transmission of data in digital format, enhancing precision and integration with modern technology systems.</p></p>
