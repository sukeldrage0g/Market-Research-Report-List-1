<p><p>The countertop burners market is primarily divided into three types: gas, electric, and induction. Gas burners use flames for direct heat, offering quick temperature control and a traditional cooking experience. Electric burners provide consistent heating through coils or smooth surfaces, ideal for versatility and ease of cleaning. Induction burners utilize magnetic fields to heat cookware directly, delivering rapid and energy-efficient cooking with precise temperature management. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, cooking habits, and kitchen setups.</p></p>
