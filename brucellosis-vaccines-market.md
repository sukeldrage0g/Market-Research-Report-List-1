<p><strong>The Brucellosis Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>S19 Vaccine</li><li>RB51 Vaccine Strain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brucellosis vaccines market primarily includes two key types: S19 and RB51 vaccine strains. The S19 vaccine, derived from the Brucella abortus strain, is primarily used in cattle and is known for its effectiveness in preventing brucellosis. However, it can cause abortion in pregnant animals. The RB51 strain, a rough mutant of Brucella abortus, is safer for pregnant livestock and provides long-lasting immunity without causing abortion. Both vaccines are crucial in controlling brucellosis in livestock, enhancing livestock health and productivity.</p></p>
